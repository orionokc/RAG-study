# Smart Notes
Smart Notes is an AI-powered study stool. After uploading their notes (pdf file), users can choose to have AI automatically generate flash cards from their notes and/or consult and ask questions to their notes, as Smart Notes trains existing LLMs on the passed in notes via RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation). Users also have the option to define how many flash cards to develop and the difficulty of the flash cards, as well.
